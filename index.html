Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Welcome to Love Date (Ngay Quan Trong). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Event data: Event names, dates, and notes you enter into the app.
  • Account information: Email and display name (only when you voluntarily link your account).
  • Device information: An anonymous device ID used to create sessions automatically — it does not personally identify you.
  • MBTI survey data: Stored locally on your device only, never sent to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide reminders for your important dates.
  • To sync your data across devices (if you are signed in).
  • To suggest relevant gifts and activities.
  • To improve the app experience.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Affiliate Links

The app contains affiliate links to products and services from our partners. When you click a link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you. All affiliate links are clearly labeled within the app.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Event data is stored locally on your device using SQLite.
  • If you link an account, data is synced to a secure server over HTTPS.
  • Authentication tokens are stored in the operating system's secure storage.

5. Your Rights

  • Access your data: All data you enter is visible directly in the app.
  • Delete your data: Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account to permanently delete all your data.
  • Export your data: Contact us by email to request a data export.

6. Push Notifications

The app sends push notifications to remind you of important dates. You can disable them at any time in Settings → Notifications or in your device's system settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the app. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of those changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

📧 support@lovedate.app

Chinh sach bao mat

Cap nhat lan cuoi: 01/01/2025

Chao mung ban den voi Love Date. Chung toi ton trong quyen rieng tu cua ban va cam ket bao ve thong tin ca nhan theo chinh sach nay.

1. Thong tin chung toi thu thap

  • Du lieu su kien: Ten su kien, ngay thang, ghi chu ban nhap vao ung dung.
  • Thong tin tai khoan: Email va ten hien thi (chi khi ban chu dong lien ket tai khoan).
  • Thong tin thiet bi: ID thiet bi an danh de tao phien tu dong, khong dinh danh ca nhan.
  • Du lieu khao sat MBTI: Luu cuc bo tren thiet bi, khong gui len server.

2. Cach chung toi su dung thong tin

  • Cung cap tinh nang nhac nho ngay quan trong.
  • Dong bo du lieu giua cac thiet bi (neu ban dang nhap).
  • Goi y qua tang va hoat dong phu hop.
  • Cai thien trai nghiem ung dung.

Chung toi khong ban thong tin ca nhan cua ban cho ben thu ba.

3. Lien ket Affiliate

Ung dung co chua lien ket affiliate den san pham va dich vu cua doi tac. Khi ban nhan vao va thuc hien mua hang, chung toi co the nhan hoa hong nho — khong ton them chi phi nao cua ban. Tat ca lien ket affiliate deu duoc danh dau ro rang trong ung dung.

4. Luu tru va bao mat du lieu

  • Du lieu su kien luu cuc bo tren thiet bi bang SQLite.
  • Neu lien ket tai khoan, du lieu dong bo len may chu bao mat qua HTTPS.
  • Ma xac thuc (token) luu trong bo nho an toan cua he dieu hanh.

5. Quyen cua ban

  • Xem du lieu: Toan bo du lieu hien thi truc tiep trong ung dung.
  • Xoa du lieu: Vao Cai dat → Vung nguy hiem → Xoa tai khoan de xoa toan bo du lieu vinh vien.
  • Xuat du lieu: Lien he chung toi qua email de yeu cau.

6. Thong bao (Push Notifications)

Ung dung gui thong bao nhac nho ngay quan trong. Ban co the tat bat cu luc nao trong Cai dat → Thong bao hoac cai dat he thong cua thiet bi.

7. Thay doi chinh sach

Chung toi co the cap nhat chinh sach nay theo thoi gian. Thay doi quan trong se duoc thong bao qua ung dung.

8. Lien he

Neu ban co cau hoi ve chinh sach bao mat, vui long lien he:

📧 support@lovedate.app
